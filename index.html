The Marketplace

By Tara Vuksanovic

You are walking through the marketplace with only a few rubles in hand. You need to buy just one important thing for your home.

You see a bike. All of the rich and materialistic are crowded around it. They are laughing with each other about all the richer places they can go. They are oblivious and greedy.

You see a bible. It rests with ease on a stand with your country's logos and posters about morals, family, and your culture. All of the old fashioned and humble stand around it. Their conversations are louder than the rich mens'.

You see a brand new phone. All the newest trends and media playing on it's small screen. Most of the crowd is around the phone, wandering soullessly. Most of them are your age.
